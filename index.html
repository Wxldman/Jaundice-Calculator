This calculator is based on the local neonatal jaundice reference chart and is intended to support, not replace, clinical judgement. Consult senior/neonatology urgently for jaundice in the first 24 hours, suspected haemolysis, sepsis, prematurity, acidosis, neurological signs, rapidly rising bilirubin, or exchange-level bilirubin.
